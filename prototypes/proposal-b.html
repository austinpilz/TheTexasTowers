Cold War Early-Warning Radar · 1955–1963

The Texas Towers

Offshore radar platforms built off the U.S. northeast coast to watch for a Soviet air attack. This archive gathers the photographs, names, and documents of the men who served — and of Texas Tower No. 4, lost to the sea.
