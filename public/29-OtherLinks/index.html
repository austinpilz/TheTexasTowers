04. Radomes.org

  • We are a 100% member-supported association of United States and Royal Canadian Air Force veterans, families and supporters. Our primary mission and purpose is:

  • To preserve the history of Cold War era air and missile defense of the North American continent.

  • To document the Air Defense radar installations, radar equipment, and personnel who served, through photographs, documents, first-hand accounts, newspaper articles, etc., and to make this documentation available to the public for historic educational purposes.

  • To promote the recognition and benefit of military veterans who served in any capacity in the air and missile defense of the North American continent.

  • To support various Radomes projects, among which are primarily the Online Air Defense Radar Museum and working towards the preservation and restoration of air defense radar installations.

05. Texastower.com

  • The Texas Tower Association was formed as a non-profit association in 1999. The membership is composed of men who had served on or who had supported these towers, their families, and any individuals who have an interest in the Texas Towers.

  • The purpose of the Association is to promote fellowship, fraternity and friendship among its members, to preserve the history of the Texas Towers, and to provide an outlet to share feelings about service associated with this unique and dangerous duty.
