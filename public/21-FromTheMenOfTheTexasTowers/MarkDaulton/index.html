Texas Tower Model

Mark Told Me A Story That An Older Gentleman Was Looking For The Model At The Museum. And A Few Hours Later He Came Back And Said He Never Found It. So Mark Got Him In A Weal Chair And Took Him To Where It Was. The Gentleman And His Wife Were Now Smiling Ear To Ear.

It's Things Like This That Should Touch All Of Our Hearts, For The Men And Woman That Serve In Our Armed Forces. Many People Now Days Don't Think About The Sacrifices The They Make To Protect The United States, And There Freedom.
