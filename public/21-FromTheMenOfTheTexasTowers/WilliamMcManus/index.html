Although The Following Images Are Not Perfect In Quality, They Do Present Information Never Seen Before About The Texas Towers.

Plans To Add X Bracing To The Texas Towers, The X Bracing Was On Tower #4, But Never Added To Towers 2 & 3. It Is Believed That The X Bracing Was The Final Design Failure That Was Implemented That Caused The Tower Number 4 To Fall Into The Ocean Killing The Remaining Personal Onboard.

I Believe The K Bracing Put Too Much Stress On The Thin Tower Legs And The Reinforced Sleeves Near The Water Line. This Caused A Crack On One Of The Legs And Allowing Ocean Water To Enter The Legs Where They Kept Fuel, And Fresh Drinking Water.
